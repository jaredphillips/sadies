What is Chili-for-Charity?

On a bi-monthly basis, Sadie's Diner presents Chili-for-Charity: a night where all proceeds go towards a worthy local cause.

A suggested donation of $10 gets you all-you-can-eat vegan chili (served with chips).

Please send any inquiries/charity recommendations to: info@sadiesdiner.com
