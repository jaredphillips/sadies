About Sadie's Diner

Since 2006 Sadie's Diner And Juice Bar has been serving up all your favorite traditional comfort foods, updated. Along with our vegetarian menu we offer fair-trade, organic coffee and a wide range of fresh juices and smoothies made with real fruit and available with protein boosts. Our eggs are free-run, our milk and cream is organic, our cheese is rennet-free and we offer vegan alternatives to all of these. Every Wednesday our coffee is 25 cents before noon. All of our take-out containers are 100% compostable and may be deposited in your green bin.

Sadie's Diner offers a warm, friendly place to meet your friends or make new ones. We are involved in our community and our neighbourhood through special events such as our bi-monthly chili-for-charity nights, our art shows and our discounts for community group members. Come check out our wall of Pez.
